The CliniquePlus Advantage

Local & International Contributions

CliniquePlus EMR is not just another EMR, Its built from real world experiences of Nigerian & foreign caregivers & administrators in private and public sectors . Its a mix of global best practices with local realities to build a system tailored for Nigeria’s unique medical landscape

Easy To Use & Modern Interface

CliniquePlus features a modern, clean interface with minimal buttons per page. Menus and actions are well-aligned and optimized to complete tasks in as few clicks as possible. This greatly increases usability, improves turn around bank and more income for the hospital/clinic.

Dedicated IT & Experienced Professionals

Software doesn't run on its own. It runs on IT infrastructure. Failure of these technologies stops the software from working. CliniquePlus EMR software has dedicated computer, network, and software engineers to implement or fix any IT challenges or customize/build any features.

Offline First, Cloud Backed Up Approach

With Nigeria’s internet challenges, depending entirely on online systems isn’t practical. It poses a lot of downtime in practice. CliniquePlus uses a 2-server approach: clinics operate locally without internet, and data is synced to the cloud for cloud access and backup whenever connectivity is available.

Extended On-site Training & Re-Training

Digital health and computer literacy are still growing in Nigeria, and without proper support, many healthcare workers may abandon software and return to paper. CliniquePlus provides up to 3 weeks of on-site training anywhere in Nigeria, with ongoing physical or remote retraining to ensure long-term adoption.

Local & International Standards Upheld

CliniquePlus complies with both local and international laws and standards, including Nigeria’s National Health Act 2014 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023. It also supports global standards like HL7, FHIR, DICOM, ICD, and SNOMED CT. We are also big on interoperability where health records can be shared with other facility either with FHIR or our custom made API’s
